The MImPG project has officially kicked off! Our interdisciplinary team from
ULB and UCLouvain gathered for the first time to set the foundations for
researching post-growth metropolitan internet access in the Brussels region.

## Goals

The project aims to study how internet infrastructure can operate sustainably
within planetary boundaries while still meeting the basic connectivity needs
of all citizens. We will explore both the societal and technical dimensions
of this challenge.

## Next Steps

Over the coming months, we will begin our first work packages focusing on
literature review and stakeholder identification in the Brussels metropolitan
area.
